ArtovaModel is based on Artova’s six years of experience and the projects of six Spirit of Artova working groups: Arabia Street Festival, Artova Film Festival, Artova Kino, Edible Arabianranta, Design Dog Park and My House Arabianranta.

Artova (Arabianranta-Toukola-Vanhakaupunki culture and neighborhood association) is a community of Arabia area people, residents, workers, entrepreneurs, students and others who are interested in the area. Artova is the most active neighborhood association in the capital region. It organizes art and cultural events, participates actively in city planning and development and creates new kinds of practices.
